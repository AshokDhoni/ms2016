About Me

photo by Tom Tian

I live in the beautiful city of Chicago and work as a Web Developer at Firebelly Design.

I'm passionate about people, equality and happiness. I want to work on projects that have a positive impact on the world beyond just making someone money.

In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, skateboarding, endurance sports, and playing music. I love to travel and go on outdoor adventures, which I often photograph and post on Flickr.

I sometimes talk on the Tight Pencils Podcast, I am the organizer of the CodePen Chicago meetups, and I occasionally teach Web Development courses and workshops at General Assembly.
